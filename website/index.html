Features

Everything in one place

Six powerful capabilities, one lightweight app.

⏬

Download & Play Offline

Queue any video or audio URL for background downloading. Full in-app player with scrubbing, background audio, Picture-in-Picture, and resume — no subscription required.

📰

Personalized News Feed

Stories ranked by trending velocity, popularity, and your interests — not a black-box algorithm. Follow topics, mute sources, and teach FinchWire what you care about.

🔔

Creator Alerts

Get notified the moment your favorite YouTube channels go live, upload new videos, or schedule streams. Per-creator controls, quiet hours, and daily notification caps.

🏠

Smart Home Dashboard

Glanceable smart tiles for live weather, stock & crypto prices, your download queue status, and top stories — all configurable and reorderable from one screen.

📺

Live TV

A curated channel guide with live YouTube stream embeds, Pluto-style. Browse channels, tap to watch, and pick up where you left off with persistent channel memory.

🔒

Private & Secure

Optional biometric + PIN app lock, JWT-based auth, and a self-hostable backend so your data stays under your control. No ads, no tracking.
